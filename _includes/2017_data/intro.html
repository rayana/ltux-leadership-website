Why Design Leaders?

Design leadership can be hard — especially if you’ve not done it before. As the design practice evolves from a nice-to-have to a key strategic value for organizations, we want to foster a community where leaders can learn from one another. We will give designers an inspiring and transformative experience that will help them learn what to learn, to build their personal tool-set of skills and values, and to grow their professional networks.

Ideal for

Senior- and lead-level practitioners and managers who want to influence, inspire, oversee design direction, and instil a culture of design within their organizations.
