Meet People Where They Are: The Importance of Empathy in Leadership

The pace of tech companies puts design managers under enormous pressure to get results as quickly as possible. There can be little time to support and encourage each team members career goals or foster a healthy collaborative environment when faced with unrealistic deadlines and hyper growth.

Sarrah talks about how design leaders are uniquely positioned to foster a healthy environment through the same practices we use to solve design problems.

  • Hiring based on strengths and weaknesses
  • Resolving conflict using empathy
  • Stop trying to convince or persuade - meet people where they are

Sarrah Vesselov

Sarrah Vesselov is a UX professional, speaker, and author with over 10 years of experience in design, development, and management. Sarrah speaks at conferences around the world on the topics of user experience and front-end development. Her co-authored book, Building Design Systems will be published by Apress in July of 2019.
