Effectory
Singel 126-130
1015 AE Amsterdam
Google Maps

Based in the heart of the Amsterdam, Effectory is Europe's largest provider of employee feedback solutions. This unique, historic venue, is the headquarters for offices in Munich and Cape Town. Effectory facilitates some of the world's biggest employee survey programs.

Unfortunately this venue is not wheelchair-accessible, but please tell us in advance if you're in a wheelchair. We have to arrange a few minor details.
